About Elim New Jerusalem Church

Who We Are

Elim New Jerusalem Church (ENJC) is a Christ-centered family where faith grows, lives are transformed, and God is glorified. We warmly welcome everyone to experience God’s presence and fellowship with us.

Jesus Christ – The Head of Our Church

We believe Jesus Christ is the foundation and leader of our church. He is our Shepherd and our Cornerstone. Everything we do is centered on glorifying Him, following His Word, and living according to His will.

Our Pastor

Messages and teachings are led by Pastor A. Karthik Daniel. Through prayer, worship, and the Word of God, we are guided to grow spiritually and serve our community faithfully.
